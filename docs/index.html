Frequently Asked Questions

What molecules can I screen?

DrugMind supports any small molecule with a known structure. Our database includes 10,847+ pre-analyzed compounds from PubChem, ChEMBL, and DrugBank. You can also input custom SMILES strings.

How accurate is the ADMET prediction?

Our ADMET models achieve 94.7% accuracy on benchmark datasets, validated against experimental data from 50,000+ compounds. Predictions include confidence intervals and applicability domain assessment.
